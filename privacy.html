MDHuntFishOutdoors Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 18, 2026 • App Version 2.2.0

MDHuntFishOutdoors ("the App") is a free iOS app built for Maryland's hunting, fishing, camping, and hiking community. This policy describes what data the App collects, how it is used, where it goes, and your rights.

We take privacy seriously. The App is designed to work offline for most features, uses no third-party advertising networks, and never sells your data.

1. Information You Can Use the App Without Providing

You can browse every map, all public lands, every campground, every fishing access site, every Appalachian Trail mile, and the full regulations library without creating an account. No email, no password, no sign-in is required for read-only use.

2. Information the App Collects

Location Data

The App requests location permission to center maps on your position and to enable trip tracking. Location data is processed on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. You may deny location access and continue to use the App with manual map panning.

Account Data (Optional)

If you choose to save favorites, create trip plans, post scouting reports, or join a shared Deer Camp, you will create a free username-based account. We collect:

  • A username you choose (no email or phone number required)
  • A password hash (we never see your plaintext password)

No real name, email address, phone number, or government ID is ever required or requested.

User-Generated Content

When you use account-gated features, the App transmits content you create to our backend servers:

  • Favorites (hunting lands, fishing spots, campgrounds, blinds, trails)
  • Trip plans and gear lists you save
  • Deer Camp journal entries, waypoints, and member lists
  • Group Camp messages and shared sessions
  • Scouting reports and forum posts
  • Photos you upload (hunt, fish, camp, hike — EXIF location metadata is stripped before upload)

Crash Reports and Analytics (Opt-In)

The App includes first-party analytics and crash reporting infrastructure. Analytics is opt-in and disabled by default — you must explicitly enable it in Settings. When enabled, we may collect anonymized usage events (screen views, feature interactions) to improve the App. No personally identifiable information is ever sent. Events are queued locally and transmitted in batches; if you are offline, nothing is sent until you reconnect.

Sentry crash reporting is wired for future activation and is currently inactive in V2.2.0.

3. Information the App Does Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any of the following:

  • Your real name, email address, phone number, or physical mailing address
  • Your contacts, calendar, or messages
  • Your browsing history outside the App
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA) — no IDFA is read or shared
  • Precise location when trip tracking is not active

4. Where Your Data Goes

Our Backend (Render)

Account data and user-generated content is transmitted over HTTPS to our API hosted at huntplan-api.onrender.com (Render Inc.). The backend stores data necessary to deliver the features you use and does not share it with third parties except as required to operate the service (e.g., database hosting).

Mapbox

The App uses Mapbox for map rendering. Mapbox may receive anonymized usage data (tile requests, session counts) per their own privacy policy. No account data or personally identifiable information is shared with Mapbox through the App.

Anthropic (AI Planner)

The AI Planner feature (optional, in Hunt and Fish modes) sends your question text to Anthropic's Claude API via our backend. Your question is not tied to your account and is not stored for training. If you do not use the AI Planner, no data is sent to Anthropic.

NOAA Tides

Fish mode fetches live tide data from NOAA's public Tides and Currents API. Requests are anonymous and contain no user information.

Maryland DNR

Hunting land, fishing access site, landowner blind, and tidal boundary data is sourced from public Maryland Department of Natural Resources ArcGIS FeatureServers and is bundled with the App at release. DNR does not receive any information about your usage.

No Advertising Networks

The App displays no advertisements and transmits no data to ad networks, attribution SDKs, or analytics SDKs such as Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, Facebook SDK, or similar.

5. Photo Uploads and EXIF Stripping

When you upload a photo (catch photo, gear photo, or scouting photo), the App removes EXIF metadata — including GPS coordinates, camera serial numbers, and timestamps — before the photo is transmitted. This prevents inadvertent disclosure of your exact location or device.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is rated for general audiences. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has created an account, please contact us and we will remove it promptly.

7. Your Rights and Controls

You can:

  • Use the App without any account
  • Deny location permission at any time via iOS Settings
  • Disable or never enable analytics
  • Delete your account and all associated data — contact us at the email below; requests are processed within 30 days
  • Request a copy of data we hold about you

8. Data Retention

Account data is retained while your account is active. Deleted accounts and their content are permanently removed from production databases within 30 days. Analytics events, if you opted in, are retained for 90 days then aggregated and deleted.

9. Security

Data is transmitted between the App and our backend over HTTPS with TLS 1.2 or higher. Passwords are hashed before storage. No security measure is perfect, but we follow industry-standard practices for a solo-developer project.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected in the Effective Date above and announced on the App's release notes. Continued use of the App after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
