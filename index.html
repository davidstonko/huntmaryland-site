Every public hunting land in Maryland, with boundaries, species, legal weapons, and 2,000 registered landowner blinds.

  • Regulation chunks linked to each property

579 DNR angler access sites, 100-segment Tidal/Non-Tidal boundary overlay, and NOAA tide integration.

  • 446 access sites + 133 stocking locations

  • Tidal boundary so you know which rules apply
  • Catch photo uploads with EXIF stripped

25 verified Maryland campgrounds — state parks, state forests, and AT backpacker shelters — with trip planning and group invites.

  • Trip planner with gear recommender
  • Group Camp shared sessions via invite link
  • Leave No Trace, bear safety, tick prevention
  • Fire policy by property

The Maryland Appalachian Trail in full detail, plus 19 state-park trails with verified distances and a 3-tier gear system.

  • 40.9 AT miles, 9 shelters, 10 trailheads
  • 12 landmarks, 10 water sources

  • AT permits, etiquette, safety resources
